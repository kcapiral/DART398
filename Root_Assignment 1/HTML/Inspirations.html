Waark

Design: Beautifully, colorfully, graphically designed. The animation emphasizes the abstract illustrations. The color palette is well chosen and matches very well with one another. The combination of serif and sans serif type works very well together. The logo is simple but still stands out on the website.

Usability: A very easy to navigate website. In order to scroll, there are various ways to move from one section of the page to another. You can either click the hamburger menu to go straight to a section, or swipe/drag up depending on what console is being used. There’s even an indication at the bottom but the usability is still very intuitive.

Creativity: Animations on the website and the gradation from one section to another makes up the creative aspect. A simple website that also stimulates the creativity of the user.

Content: Straightforward and informative and accompanied by illustration, the website simply explains the design firm

Nacache Design

Design: Very simple and minimalistic. Accents of color makes the website less monochromatic. The overlaying of type and imagery makes it not flat. The combination of realism, typography, basic shapes and color makes the composition dynamic.

Usability: Navigation is straightforward, having a simple menu on top and an intuitive vertical scrolling on the left. It’s even indicated for users on the left by colored dots the scrolling function and as an indication of what page the user is on.

Creativity: Not very creative in terms of the concept but the animations of how the elements come together is interesting. Especially when moving from one page to another, the animation for transition is colorful, graphic and is a nice touch to the simple website.

Content: Information regarding the designer is included on the website. Everything is straightforward and nothing complicated as it is a portfolio website.

nacachedesign.com

Thing of Wonder

Design: Graphics and colors are vibrant and eye-catching. Typography works well with the illustrations.

Usability: Very easy to use. The menu is easily accessible on any screen size and very straightforward. There is nothing complicated, just simple scrolling and clicking.

Creativity: Illustrations and animations are unique. The use of integrating the graphics with the text and layout makes it dynamic.

Content: All the information needed can be found on the website and more, such as fun quotes, excerpts and keywords. Information is accompanied with either illustration or images which gives a better understanding of the content.
